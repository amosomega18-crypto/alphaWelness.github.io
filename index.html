Questions Fréquentes

Everything you need to know

Do you coach in English or French?
Both. I’m a fully bilingual coach — fluent in English and French. You choose your preferred language and I adapt entirely. Many of my clients are English-speaking expats in France who simply want a trainer they can communicate with clearly.
How does online coaching work?
Your programme lives in the Everfit app on your phone or laptop. Each week you receive your workouts with video demonstrations, track your progress, and communicate with me directly in the app. We also do a weekly video check-in to review your progress.
Do I need gym equipment?
No. I build programmes around what you have access to — whether that’s a fully equipped gym, a set of dumbbells at home, or just your bodyweight. The programme adapts to your situation, not the other way around.
I’m based outside of Paris — does that matter?
Not at all. My coaching is 100% online, so it works wherever you are in France or internationally. I have clients in Paris, the South of France, and across Europe — all coached remotely via the app and video calls.
What makes you different from a fitness app?
A fitness app gives you a generic plan and zero accountability. I give you a fully personalised programme that evolves every 4 weeks, direct access to me when you need support, and holistic coaching — training, nutrition, and recovery — that creates lasting change.
What’s the minimum commitment?
Monthly programmes starting at €300/month. I recommend a minimum of 3 months to see real transformation. We discuss everything on the free discovery call — no pressure, no obligation.
